About Bandmate

Bandmate is a social networking web app designed to promote creativity in musicians by helping them meet and collaborate. Users can easily search through musicians by instrument played, location, experience level and many other filters. The simplified search for talented musicians speeds up the process of finding new members, helping to put bands back on the stage and in the studio. Besides finding new band members or just someone to jam with, Bandmate also allows users to promote themselves as instructors.
